WHY JOIN THE TEAM?

Opportunity, flexibility and new challenges every day are just a few of the reasons you’ll be happy you came on board.

When you work at Tim Hortons, you’ll feel right at home. It’s a fun, flexible environment where guests are your neighbours, where co-workers are your friends and where simple acts make for a lasting impression.

We’d love for you to join the team. Because it’s the people that make Tims, Tims. And we’re looking for people like you.

RESTAURANT TEAM MEMBER

Bringing your energy and passion to work every day helps make coming to Tims the best part of someone else’s.

DESIGNATED TRAINER

Put your supportive nature to work. Train, coach and build a strong team with the skills and knowledge they need to excel. Their success is your success.

TEAM SUPERVISOR

Lead by example and encourage your team to make guests their highest priority. Share ideas, look for opportunities to grow the business and empower your team to make it right.

ASST. RESTAURANT MANAGER

Use your enthusiasm to teach, train and guide your team to uphold our high standards of service and quality. Use your expertise to support the Manager and Owner, and to make guests feel welcome every day.

RESTAURANT MANAGER

Bring your expertise and vision to the table to build a successful business. Recruit and lead your dynamic team. Promote open communication, respect for others and a strong work ethic. Establish a fast pace and lead the way by setting goals and exceeding them.

THE TIM HORTONS SCHOLARSHIP PROGRAM

Earn a $1,000 scholarship.

Let us help get you there.
There are 220 scholarships to be awarded to eligible Tim Hortons Team Members, their children or grandchildren.

ABOUT THE SCHOLARSHIP PROGRAM

  • 200 scholarships in Canada and 20 scholarships in the US are available, each valued at $1,000.
  • Previous winners and applicants may be eligible to apply.

HOW TO APPLY

  • Submit proof of volunteer and/or community service and/or leadership hours (15 hours only).
  • Write an essay of at least 500 words on a set topic.

BASIC ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS

  • Must be a Tim Hortons Team Member or the child or grandchild (biological, step or adopted) of a Team Member.
  • Must be legally eligible to work in Canada or the US (depending on the country you or your qualifying relative resides in).
  • Must be accepted to an accredited or registered post-secondary institution for part-time or full-time studies within the scholarship year.

The 2017 program is now closed. The next application period will be open from February to April of 2018.
