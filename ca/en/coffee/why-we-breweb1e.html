APPRECIATE A FRESH CUP

For over 50 years we know better than anyone that Canadians are amazing people. That’s why we wake up early on cold winter mornings; stay late when others have gone home and why we make sure every cup is made fresh.

IS IN THE DETAILS.

A lot of love goes into crafting each and every cup of Tim Hortons Coffee. In fact, we’ve been obsessing over the details since first opening our doors back in 1964. From sourcing high quality beans to roasting under the watchful eye of coffee masters and brewing in small batches – we do it all to ensure each cup tastes
true to the blend you love.
